Privacy Policy

Snazzy Paws Grooming takes your privacy very seriously.

We are committed to safeguarding your personal information that you share with us and using it responsibly.

Information We Collect

We may collect some personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and pet information when you submit booking or contact forms on our website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Manage and confirm your appointments
  • Respond to queries you may have for your booking
  • Improve the services and overall experience for our customers

Data Protection

All personal information is stored securely and never shared with third parties except where required by law. We follow best practices to protect your data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please get in touch at support@snazzypaws.com or call us on 0800 900 PAWS.
